The Coding Journey Begins

11/10/2021

In a few short days I shall be taking the first steps on a path to becoming a Full-Stack Developer via the Coder Academy Bootcamp. If someone had asked me 10 months ago "What is a full-stack developer and how do you become one?", I wouldn't have known what to tell them and if it wasn't for my fabulously insightful wife, I probably would have never known. It was she who sent me a link to the Coder Academy course page with the simple message, "This looks like something you'd be good at" and after diligent research, I was of the same opinion.

The application process for Coder Academy went into quite alot of detail. It seems that they want to make sure that the course participant have both the aptitude to code and the ability to translate the aquired skills to a real-world application.

I am delighted that I was accepted into the coding bootcamp and will soon embark on a 10 month learning journey. My determination to succeed knows no bounds so ultimately I see no limits to what I can achieve post this course. I'll keep everyone updated with my progress along the way via this blog.
